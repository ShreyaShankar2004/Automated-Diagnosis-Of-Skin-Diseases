About Us

Our Mission

Bridging AI with Indian Dermatology

AI-DERM India was established in 2021 by a collective of Indian dermatologists and AI engineers from premier institutes like IIT Bombay and AIIMS Delhi. We aim to make quality skin consultations affordable and accessible across every corner of India.

Our goal is to empower citizens from metros to rural villages with instant, trustworthy skin analysis using AI and mobile technology.
